WHAT WE BELIEVE ABOUT THE KINGDOM

Our Church Believes

We believe in the existence of the only one true and living God who exists in three persons. The Father, the Son and the Holy Spirit.

John 1:1,14;10:30;14:26,15:26

We believe that Jesus Christ is the Son of God, begotten of the Holy Spirit, died on the cross, buried, and rose again on the third day and ascended to heaven.

Luke 1:26, 22:20, 24:45

We believe that the Holy Spirit is God who existed since the creation was created and He deposits power in the lives of believers.

Acts 1:8, Genesis 1:1

We believe that praying in tongues is the first sign of baptism of the Holy Spirit and the full endowment of the Holy Spirit in the life of a believer.

Mark 16:17, Acts 2:4

We believe that when a person hears the gospel and puts their faith in Jesus Christ by accepting His Lordship over their life, they become a born again.

2 Cor 5:7, Rom 10:9-10, Eph 1:7
